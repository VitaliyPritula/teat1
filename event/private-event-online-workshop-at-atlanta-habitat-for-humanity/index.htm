Whether she's leading a small workshop or speaking at a large gathering, Lindsey Hardegree's energy and enthusiasm are contagious! She is an experienced nonprofit executive in Metro Atlanta; Lindsey has worked at both small and large organizations and has seen a wide range of workplace configurations through her consulting services. Holding advanced degrees in both management and theology, Lindsey is uniquely poised to speak on productivity and balance with groups of all kinds. Trainings for small groups of friends, public workshops on the KonMari Method®, Lunch & Learn presentations, and keynote speaking on topics like productivity, work/life balance, and intentional living – Lindsey is available to work with you on a customized event!
